Car Trend Dashboard

Welcome to the Car Trend Dashboard, your ultimate tool for exploring car trends. This dashboard is powered by a dataset of over 200,000 cars, which I scraped and meticulously cleaned to ensure reliable and high-quality insights.

What makes this dashboard truly unique is its interactive features. Select any car, and you'll see its image along with its official video, thanks to the integration of Google Image and Video APIs. It's not just data—it’s an immersive experience!

To enhance your analysis, I added a geographical dimension by including longitude and latitude information. This allows you to visualize car trends across different locations, providing a fresh perspective on the data.

This dashboard is perfect for anyone looking to analyze the car market, uncover trends, and even find dealer details directly from the dashboard. Whether you’re a data enthusiast or a car lover, this tool is designed to deliver actionable insights.
